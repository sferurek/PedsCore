# WHO Growth Data License Policy

The PedsCore source code is licensed under the MIT License.

WHO growth data, tables, publications and related materials are not licensed
under the PedsCore MIT License. They must be used under the applicable WHO
license or terms for the specific official source file.

For WHO publications, the expected license is commonly:

CC BY-NC-SA 3.0 IGO

unless the official file, publication or WHO page states a different license or
terms.

Policy for PedsCore:

- Use WHO growth data only for non-commercial purposes unless WHO permission
  allows otherwise.
- Provide attribution to the World Health Organization.
- Keep WHO data and derived data under the applicable WHO license or compatible
  share-alike terms.
- Do not relicense WHO data as MIT.
- Do not use the WHO logo.
- Do not imply endorsement, approval or sponsorship by WHO.
- Include an adaptation disclaimer for normalized, transformed, translated or
  derived data.
- Confirm the specific license for each official file before importing it.

Commercial use of WHO materials may require permission from WHO.
