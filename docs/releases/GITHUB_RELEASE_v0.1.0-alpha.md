# PedsCore v0.1.0-alpha

PedsCore v0.1.0-alpha is the first public alpha release of PedsCore: an open-source pediatric and neonatal clinical tools library with evidence traceability, bilingual web UI and no clinical data storage.

Public web app:

https://sferurek.github.io/PedsCore/

Repository:

https://github.com/sferurek/PedsCore

## Highlights

- 79 pediatric and neonatal tools cataloged.
- 17 tools fully implemented with active calculation or informational rule output.
- 1 partially implemented module: WHO Growth.
- WHO Growth includes official WHO 0-5 indicators and partial WHO Growth Reference 2007 5-19 BMI-for-age and height-for-age support.
- Printable SVG growth charts generated by PedsCore.
- Written percentile labels: P3, P15, P50, P85 and P97.
- Visible patient point on WHO Growth charts.
- Evidence traceability, visible references and implementation status across the catalog.
- Static GitHub Pages web app.
- No backend.
- No login.
- No clinical data storage.
- No clinical form values or calculation results are sent to analytics.

## WHO Growth

The WHO Growth module is available in `partially_implemented` status.

Current scope:

- WHO Child Growth Standards 0-5:
  - BMI-for-age.
  - Weight-for-age.
  - Length/height-for-age.
  - Head circumference-for-age.
  - Weight-for-length.
  - Weight-for-height.
- WHO Growth Reference 2007 5-19 partial scope:
  - BMI-for-age.
  - Height-for-age.
- Official WHO LMS data under separate WHO data licensing.
- PedsCore-generated SVG charts, not copied WHO chart images.
- Native browser print workflow.
- Guided age input.

The module remains partial while final maintainer review, interpolation policy and remaining 5-19 scope are completed.

## Limitations

- Public alpha: content, UX and wording remain under active review.
- Not every cataloged tool has active calculation.
- Pending tools remain blocked until source, table/formula, exact variant, tests, safe wording and licensing gates are satisfied.
- WHO Growth does not provide nutritional diagnoses or treatment recommendations.
- No toxicology tools are included in this phase.
- No backend, login, account system or clinical data persistence exists.

## Safety Disclaimer

PedsCore is an educational and informational reference for healthcare professionals. It does not diagnose, recommend treatment or replace clinical judgment, local protocols, institutional policies, specialist consultation or individual patient assessment.

Do not enter or submit patient-identifiable information in the app, issues, pull requests or examples.
